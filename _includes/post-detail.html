Podcats: A passion project

Podcats is a passion project to help find better podcasts. Made together with Niels de Hoog, we sought to build a community forum where people could reccomend podcasts to eachother. Having found that we found out about most podcasts via friends, we simply sought to create a digital hub for the practice.
